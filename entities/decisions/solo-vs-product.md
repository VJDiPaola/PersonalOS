# Solo vs Product

## Context

The open source CLI tool is getting traction. Need to decide whether to keep it as a personal project or invest in making it a proper product with docs, support, and a community.

## Considerations

- Solo: less overhead, more freedom, can pivot easily
- Product: more impact, potential revenue, but higher commitment
- Could start solo and grow into a product later

## Status

Still gathering feedback from early users before deciding.
